What we do different & Why it is Better.

Usually
You either try using a service like Shopify, Wix or WordPress where you then have to spend time trying to convert your ideas into a workinng website. In addition you will have to sign up and manage hosting, domains, security, updates and more. The other common option is working with a web development firm that will handle some or all of the headaches for you as long as you work within their system/supoort process. These firms have have large upfront fees or deposits as well.

Our Process
We believe that your time is better spend managing your business and not developing a website. We make websites simple by taking over every aspect of the process for you, that is our business.

After talking about your business and uncovering what you would like from a new business website, we get to work implementing your ideas... continue reading to hear more about how our process really makes websites simple.

Development, Design and Launch
After discussing your website goals and design preferences, we start by researching things like keywords, search engine competition and identifying a target audience. This guides our final designs, ensuring that the final website we deliver actualizes the ideas you had for your website and helps drive future growth.

Maintaining your Website
Your website is up and running, now what? It would be nice to 'set it and forget it' but rarely is this an option for business websites. Every website we manage is constantly monitored for unplanned downtimes, crashes or attacks. High priority issues will be communicated ASAP while minor issues will be included in a monthly report including information about routine updates and maintenance... such as backup status,changes in website performance (speed), broken links, keyword rankings and more.

Not only do we automatically fix any issues we identify, any updates or content changes you want to make to the website can usually be completed within 24 hours.

Evolve & Grow with Additional Development
Ready for a big change? It might be time to redesign an existing website or maybe you need to include the ability for customers to order online... no one is ordering and you need to drive more visitors through Social Media Marketing? Yah. We can help with that too.

This is the fun part where we can really get creative and work on implementing whatever new features or applications you like.
