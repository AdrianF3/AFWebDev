EctorGrow.com

This website helps users organize and manage their personal cannabis grow. Allowing users to 'add' information about their plants, growing spaces and more. This allowed users to track the development of their plants and manage it accordingly. I originally had two goals for this project, develop a tool that would help me manage my person grow and to further my development skills.

When I had a rough working version, I decided to build the functionality for individual users, login/logout and more added features like user customized nutrient charts. While planning out a bit of a redesign and adding features, I decided to refactor the project using React.

Developed with: HTML, CSS, JS, PHP, SQL
