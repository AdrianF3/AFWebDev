F.A.Q.

Routine vs. Priority Maintenance

Every website we develop is built with reliability in mind and is monitored 24x7 for any potential downtime or errors so that any issues can be automtically addressed. Additionally, with Priority Maintenance, we perform monthly audits of your website to identify any potential issues with SEO, broken links or other issues that could impact your websites overall performance.

Unlimitied Routine Edits

Unlimited Routine Edits gives you the ability to make updates to text and minor style adjustments. This does not include redesigning any pages or making significant layout changes.

Routine Edits are intended to keep the content on your website relevant and useful for your website visitors, such as sharing new store hours on your website, updates to pricing or contact information.
